Tribute to Lionel Messi

Football Legend and GOAT

Here's a time line of Lionel Messi's life:

  • 1987: Lionel Messi was born on June 24, 1987, in Rosario, Argentina.
  • 1995: Messi joined the youth team of his local club, Newell's Old Boys, showcasing his prodigious talent from a young age.
  • 2000: Diagnosed with a growth hormone deficiency, Messi moved to Barcelona, Spain, to join FC Barcelona's youth academy, La Masia, after the club offered to cover his medical expenses.
  • 2004: Messi made his official first-team debut for FC Barcelona at the age of 17, becoming the youngest player to represent the club at that time.
  • 2005-2006: Messi helped Barcelona win the UEFA Champions League, La Liga, and the FIFA Club World Cup, marking the beginning of his era of dominance.
  • 2009: Messi won his first FIFA Ballon d'Or, marking the start of a string of individual awards and records.
  • 2011:Led by Messi, Barcelona achieved a historic feat, winning the UEFA Champions League, La Liga, and the FIFA Club World Cup in a single calendar year.
  • 2012: Messi surpassed Gerd Muller's record for the most goals in a calendar year, finishing with 91 goals.
  • 2014: Messi guided Argentina to the FIFA World Cup final, but they were defeated by Germany.
  • 2015: Messi secured his fifth FIFA Ballon d'Or, becoming the first player in history to achieve such a feat.
  • 2017: Messi married his longtime partner, Antonela Roccuzzo, in a ceremony in Rosario.
  • 2021: Due to financial constraints faced by FC Barcelona, Messi left the club, eventually signing with Paris Saint-Germain (PSG) in August.
  • 2022: Messi continued to showcase his brilliance with PSG, contributing to the team's pursuit of domestic and international honors.
